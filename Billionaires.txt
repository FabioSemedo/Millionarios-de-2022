citizenship
gender
birth_date
last_name
first_name

residence_region - where defined (No subdivision if not)
birth_year
birth_month
birth_day
cpi_country - country price index
cpi_change_country - country price index change
gdp_country - country gross domestic product
g_tertiary_ed_enroll - gross tertiary education enrollment
g_primary_ed_enroll - gross primary education enrollment country
life_expectancy
tax_revenue
tax_rate
country_pop - country population
country_lat - country latitude
